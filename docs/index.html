1

Move Around

Use WASD or Arrow Keys to navigate through the game world. Mobile players can use the on-screen controls.

2

Interact with NPCs

Press E (or the action button on mobile) to talk to characters and access different opportunities.

3

Manage Your Inventory

Press I to open your inventory and check your stats, items, and progress.

4

Make Decisions

Choose your educational path, career moves, and life decisions that will shape your future.
